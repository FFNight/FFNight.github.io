TARTFE

Once upon a time. One guy saw The Amazing Race ® on TV and though "Hey, why don't we set up games like these so that everyone can play?", thus, TARTFE was born. After 10 (seriously, we counted) long years, TARTFE is still alive and kicking, and ready for your participation during Chinese New Year 2017.

History of TARTFE

Actually, none of the four original members or the "TARTFE Committee" can recall many of the past events. Most of them are part of a forgotten history and will most likely be lost unless photographic evidence can be found.

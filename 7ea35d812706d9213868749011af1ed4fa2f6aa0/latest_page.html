Electric breaker fixed by genius technician free of charge!

AUSTIN PERDANA. An electrical breaker fault in Austin Perdana has rendered one of the room without any air-conditioning. Making life difficult for the many residents as well as visitors to the house especially combined with the hot and humid weather of Malaysia.

Luckily, an unlikely visitor who also happens to be a gifted technician managed to fix the issue with ease. According to eyewitness report, the man managed to fix the fault in a record breaking 17 minutes. One eyewitness commented "I can't believe he finished it so fast!". Another eyewitness who is impressed with his technical skills said that "We need people like this in my company."

We caught up with this mysterious technician around 3.03p.m. It turns out this mysterious technician is no other than Mr. Tee Yook Chai, who also owns the company Nonstopower Sdn. Bhd. which specializes in power supply and battery services. "I never expected any kind of fanfare over this, it is just a simple fix." the man responded to our inquiry, "Anyone can do this if they know the basics." He denied any further questions from our reporter, citing that "the things I've done are not worthy of praise". We are proud to have such a humble and skilled member in our family.

The house owner Mdm. Tee Geok Choo is very impressed with Mr. Tee's skills, "He managed to fix it without spending any money, which is always welcome in this family. We look forward to work with him again in the future."

Editor's Note

Welcome to Tee Family Club.

Last year we made a website to commemorate the next step in our annual TARTFE games, which you can find here. However, from our survey responses after the event it is obversed that 16% of our respondents does not know the website exist.

After analysis we concluded that the lack of promotion as well as the promotional methods are the main reasons for the lack of awareness of the website. I have also recieved several messages suggesting we should go back to the "old ways" - the posters or word of mouth - to spread the message which I will try to do so in promoting this new site as the place to gather information regarding our family events and matters.

I will try to update the site as frequent as possible, so it would be great if you can stop by every day and leave some feedback in our family group on Whatsapp.

Ting Yi, signing off.
